| Metric                 | Bronze | Silver | Gold   |
| ---------------------- | ------ | ------ | ------ |
| Total Questions Asked  | 10     | 50     | 100    |
| Total Answers Given    | 10     | 50     | 100    |
| Total Question Upvotes | 10     | 50     | 100    |
| Total Answer Upvotes   | 10     | 50     | 100    |
| Total Views Received   | 1000   | 10000  | 100000 |

**Example:**

Suppose a user has the following stats:

- Total Questions Asked: 5 (not enough to get any badge)
- Total Answers Given: 20 (**`Bronze`** for Total Answers Given)
- Total Upvotes on Questions: 25 (**`Bronze`** for Total Question Upvotes)
- Total Upvotes on Answers: 90 (**`Silver`** for Total Upvotes on Answers)
- Total Views Received: 880 (not enough to get any badge)
